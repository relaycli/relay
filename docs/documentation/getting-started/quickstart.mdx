## Build your first e-mail app (hosted cloud)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Invoxflow account">
    [Sign-up](https://invoxflow.com/auth/register) with Google / Microsoft or e-mail+password.
    You’ll land on the **Dashboard → Accounts** screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grab your mailbox credentials">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Gmail">
        * Head over to [App passwords](https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords)
        * Enter an App name (e.g. "Invoxflow")
        * Click on "Create" and save the password

        <Info>
          If you encounter any issue, make sure that App passwords are available for your account ([Gmail Help](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/185833)).
          As of July 2025, all you need is to turn [2-Step verification](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/185839) on.
        </Info>
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Outlook">
        Follow the steps in [How to get and use app passwords](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/account-billing/how-to-get-and-use-app-passwords-5896ed9b-4263-e681-128a-a6f2979a7944)
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Custom IMAP host">
        Copy your account password
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect the inbox">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Dashboard">
        * Head over to [Connect email account](https://invoxflow.com/accounts/new)
        * Enter your email credentials
        * Click on "Connect account"
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="API">
        Hit the endpoint to [connect your account](/api-reference/accounts/connect-account)
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fetch your latest emails">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Dashboard">
        On your [account page](https://invoxflow.com/accounts), click on "View details" of your mailbox. Your recent email should be displayed here
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="API">
        Hit the endpoint to [fetch your account's emails](/api-reference/inbox/fetch-emails)
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an API key">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Dashboard">
        Go to [API Keys](https://invoxflow.com/settings/api-keys), click on "Create new API key", enter a name (e.g. "Email digestor"), set an expiration date, and click "Create key".
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="API">
        Hit the endpoint to [fetch your account's emails](/api-reference/keys/create-key)
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    <Warning>Save the API key, as it will never be possible to show it again!</Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build an email digest (optional)">
    Check [API reference](/api-reference) and build something awesome.
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Self-hosting (docker-compose)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install prerequisites">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Linux">
        * [Docker](https://docs.docker.com/engine/install/)
        * [Git](https://git-scm.com/downloads/linux)
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="macOS">
        * [Docker Desktop](https://docs.docker.com/desktop/setup/install/mac-install/)
        * [Git](https://git-scm.com/downloads/mac)
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Windows">
        * [WSL](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/wsl/install)
        * [Docker Desktop](https://docs.docker.com/desktop/setup/install/windows-install/)
        * [Git](https://git-scm.com/downloads/win)
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clone our GitHub repo">
    ```bash
    git clone https://github.com/invoxflow/invoxflow.git
    cd invoxflow
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a configuration file">
    Copy `.env.example` to `.env`:
    ```bash
    cp .env.example .env
    ```
    Edit the values of
    ```dotenv
    BACKEND_SUPERADMIN_EMAIL=
    BACKEND_SUPERADMIN_PWD=
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the stack">
    ```bash
    make start-local
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy on your own VPS (optional)">
    Coming soon
  </Step>
</Steps>
